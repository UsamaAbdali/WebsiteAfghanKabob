Our History

Since 2010, Afghan Kabob has been the definition of excellence making it one of the finest establishments in North America. Conveniently located minutes from all airport hotels and the International Centre, Afghan Kabob is the ideal experience for all occasions. Enjoy the relaxed yet luxurious atmosphere and the finest in steak and seafood. Bask in the rich tradition of culinary art and make Afghan Kabob your next dining destination.

Our Menu

We source our beef from specific farms and regions, bringing these specialised farm breeds to our small humble restaurant. We have always aimed to bring the “farm to table” dining experience to our customers whereby they can find out more about the authentic they are having.

Afghan Kabob on Overlea is one of five in the GTA, serving up Afghani Halal cuisine to bustling lunch and dinner crowds. Bamiyan's meat is marinated for 24 hours and served in wraps, salads, and kabobs alongside rice and naan. Everything is prepared fresh each day on location.
